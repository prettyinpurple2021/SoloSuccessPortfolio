Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built by a solo founder who knows exactly what wastes your time and what doesn't.

One-Click Expansion

Drop in a URL, paste text, or upload a doc. Content Factory builds a full content calendar around it instantly.

Platform Optimiser

Each piece is rewritten for the specific platform — Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, newsletters, TikTok scripts — each optimised for native engagement.

Brand Voice Lock

Upload 3 examples of your best content and Content Factory learns your tone, style, and vocabulary. Every output sounds like you.

Repurposing Engine

Turn old content into new. Point it at last year's blog posts and get a fresh batch of social content without writing a word.

Schedule & Publish

Connect your social accounts and schedule directly from Content Factory. One workflow from idea to published.

Analytics Loop

Track which content performs best and feed that back into future generation — the engine gets smarter the more you use it.
