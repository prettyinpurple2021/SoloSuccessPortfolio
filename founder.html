The Story

How It Started.

→ Searched everywhere.
→ Found nothing.
→ Built it herself.
✦ 2024

"I needed something that didn't exist yet."

I was in the middle of building my own SaaS product — solo, no co-founder, no team, no blueprint. I had the technical skills and the vision, but I kept running into the same wall: I didn't know what to do next.

I needed something to help me stay organized, prioritize intelligently, and actually move toward launch. Not a generic project management tool. Not another course that assumed I had a team. Something built for exactly the situation I was in — one person, limited time, unlimited ambition.

I searched the entire internet. It wasn't there.

I looked everywhere. Product Hunt, Reddit, Google, app directories, startup forums — nothing built specifically for solo founders trying to launch a SaaS. Everything was either designed for teams, designed for enterprises, or just too generic to be useful when you're operating alone.

The solo founder experience is fundamentally different. You're wearing every hat simultaneously. You have no one to delegate to, no one to sanity-check you, and no margin for wasted time. Generic tools just don't cut it.

So I stopped searching and started building.

That frustration became fuel. If the tool I needed didn't exist, then there were probably thousands of other solo founders who needed it too. Being an engineering student and a lifelong DIY person, I had the skills to do something about it.

SoloSuccess Solutions was born from that gap. Not from a business plan or an investor deck — from a genuine, personal problem that nobody had solved. Seven products, one company, one mission: give solo founders the tools that used to require a whole team.
